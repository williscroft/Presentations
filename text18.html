If you didn't fix it, it ain't fixed.

  • Check that it's really fixed.
  • Check it's really your fix that fixed it.
  • Know it never just goes away by itself.
    • Use the make it fail techniques
    • If you have to ship it, add a trap to catch it in the field and record all the things you want.
  • Fix the cause
  • Fix the process (so it won't happen again).
