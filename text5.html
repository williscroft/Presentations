Make it fail pt 2

  • Record everything and find the signature of the intermittent bugs.
  • Don't trust statistics too much.
    • 50% of the time doing X matters… it might not matter.
  • Know that “that” can happen.
    • The flavour of ice-cream matters.
  • Never throw away a debugging tool.
    • The “test” tool is often the most use to operators.
