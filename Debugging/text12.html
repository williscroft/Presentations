Keep an audit trail

  • Write down what you did
    • In what order
    • And what happened
  • The devil is in the details.
    • Plaid shirt crashed the video compressor
  • Correlate events
    • It made a bad sound for 4 seconds starting at 3:58 beats “it made a bad sound”
    • Sync your #$%#$ clocks if distributed.
    • Logs need timestamps, or engineers need lynching.
    • Redirect stdout/err through log4
