Quit thinking and look

  • See the failure
    • What you see from the bug is the result of the failure
    • The actual failure is elsewhere
    • If you guess and fix your guess you'll get nowhere
    • Observe the system when it fails and see the actual bad parameter to the call, the network timeout etc.
    • Making the observation might be slower than guessing but at least it's right.
    • We can speculate endlessly without data.
