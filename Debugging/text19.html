Stochastic or Bayesian profiling

  • Good for performance bottlenecks.
  • Remember how to do it..
    • Record stack traces from the system while it's being slow.
      • Kill -3 <pid> on a JRE will dump it to stderr.
        • You are logging with a time stamp aren't you!
    • Repeat 3-5 times.
      • It's important to do it human-timed to be really random.
    • Compare all sets of stack traces.
      • Whatever is in most of them is your problem
  • When every section of code is equally likely, it stops working.
    • So either it's all good, or all bad.
